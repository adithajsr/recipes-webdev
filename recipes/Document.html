Aditha's Famous Pizza Recipe

Description

This recipe produces genuine, authentic Italian-style pizza. It is loved worldwide and you will love it too!

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Put sauce on pizza base
  2. Put a generous amount of cheese on top of sauce
  3. Put the pepperoni on top of the cheese
