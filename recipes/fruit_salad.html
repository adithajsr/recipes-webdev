Aditha's Famous Fruit Salad Recipe

Description

This recipe produces a delicious fruit salad that is loved worldwide. It is healthy, easy to make and tastes amazing.

Ingredients

  • 1 Apple
  • 1 Orange
  • 1 Banana
  • 3 Strawberries

Steps

  1. Wash fruits
  2. Cut up each fruit into biteable chunks
  3. Place all fruits in a bowl and mix
